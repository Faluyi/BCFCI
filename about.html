About BCFCI

Welcome to our Bethel Covenant Family Church Int'l, where we embrace the boundless love and illuminating light of God through the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ. At BCFCI, we strive to embody His message of compassion, forgiveness, and grace in all that we do. Our congregation is a diverse tapestry of individuals united by a common faith and a shared commitment to spreading the love of Christ to all corners of the world. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we seek to shine brightly as beacons of hope and love in our community, nation and beyond. Every sermon delivered from our pulpit is rooted in the timeless truths found within the Holy Scriptures, aiming to inspire, uplift, and guide each member on their spiritual journey. We believe that by following the example set by Jesus, we can become vessels of His love, bringing healing and reconciliation to a broken world. Whether you are seeking solace, spiritual growth, or simply a welcoming community to call home, we invite you to join us as we reflect the love and light of God in all that we do. Together, let us walk in the footsteps of Jesus, spreading His message of love and salvation to all who will hear.

Our Vision

To show forth the light and love of God to all mankind through the preaching of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To build a place of encounter with God that will bring about a resurgence of hope and a new lease of life by the efficacy of the word of God and the impartation of grace by the spirit of God.

To preach the gospel and expand the kingdom of God in every area of life.

To re-enact a covenant relationship with our Lord Jesus Christ that we may ultimately become His people and He, our God.
